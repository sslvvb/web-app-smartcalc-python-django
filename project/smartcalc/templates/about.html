About Calculator App

Welcome to the Calculator App, a simple web-based calculator that allows you to perform basic arithmetic operations.

Features:

  • Addition: Perform addition of two or more numbers.
  • Subtraction: Perform subtraction of two numbers.
  • Multiplication: Perform multiplication of two or more numbers.
  • Division: Perform division of two numbers.

Instructions:

  1. Enter the numbers you want to calculate in the input fields.
  2. Select the desired operation from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click on the "Calculate" button to see the result.
  4. The result will be displayed below the button.
